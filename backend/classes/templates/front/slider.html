Marybeth Wilde, Dance Director

Marybeth has danced for 20 years. She began her career at The Academy of Ballet where she was trained under the direction of Mark Lanham, Stephanie Parker, Carrie Write, Ann Royal and Phyllis Whitaker. In high school, she performed the lead role of the Dove from Hansel and Gretel, Princess Florine in the Bluebird Pas de Deux from Sleeping Beauty, The Autumn Fairy from Cinderella, the Scarecrow in The Wizard of Oz, and Clara from the Nutcracker. She then went on to BYU-Idaho to receive her degree as a Registered Nurse. During nursing school she was a member of the Upper Valley Civic Ballet Company in Rexburg, Idaho where she performed in the ballet, Carmen. She has taught and assisted ballet for all ages for 9 years. Along with ballet, Marybeth has been trained in jazz, modern, tap, irish, folk, and contemporary dance. Marybeth is married to Andy Wilde and they have two adorable daughters. She currently works at Utah Valley Hospital and she and her family live in Payson, Utah.

Ann Kotyk, Ballet Instructor

Ann started her career at the age of eight at The Academy of Ballet and has been dancing for a total of 16 years. She was taught under the direction of Carrie Write, Ann Royal and Phyllis Whitaker. She performed as the White Dove from Swan Lake and Princess Florine in the Bluebird Pas de Deux from Sleeping Beauty. Ann has taken many forms of dance including jazz, modern, irish, contemporary, and ballroom. She has assisted teaching for 4 years and is looking forward to soon taking on her own students. Ann graduated from Utah Valley University in Deaf Studies as an American Sign Language Interpreter, where she was also a member of the UVU’s Bronze Ballroom Team. Ann is married to Zack Kotyk and has an adorable little girl who occasionally makes her way to ballet class with her mom.

Jessica Sanders, Ballet Instructor

Jessica grew up in the Seattle area and has been dancing for a total of 15 years. She started at the age of seven competing in jazz, lyrical, hip hop and tap. At the age of eleven, she centered her training on ballet under the instruction of Michiko Black at MK ballet and then a few years later under Kevin Kaiser at Evergreen City Ballet. Some of her favorite roles include Cupid from Don Quixote, Dew Drop from the Nutcracker and the Crystal Fountain Fairy and Sliver Jewel from Sleeping Beauty. She has performed with BYU’s Theatre Ballet Company for two seasons now and toured California and the Pacific Northwest with them. In addition, she enjoys choreographing her own work, creating her first piece as a senior in high school and continuing throughout college. Jessica is currently pursuing an accounting degree at BYU with a Ballet minor. She served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints to Kyiv, Ukraine and was just recently married to McKay Sanders. She has assisted and substituted ballet classes for two years now and is excited for the opportunity to have her own students.
