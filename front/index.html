Petit Ballet Academy's Mission

Our dance curriculum establishes an environment that fosters love and enjoyment for dance. It creates a strong foundation in ballet while building on important character qualities the children will use throughout their lives. Ballet should be fun, and this can best be accomplished within a structured and relaxed environment. We are excited to share our enjoyment of ballet with everyone!

Meet Our Staff

"What the teacher is, is more important than what she teaches."

Karl A. Menninger

Marybeth Wilde, Ballet Director

Marybeth has danced for 20 years. She began her career at The Academy of Ballet where she was trained under the direction of Mark Lanham, Stephanie Parker, Carrie Write, Ann Royal and Phyllis Whitaker. In high school she performed the lead role of the Dove from Hansel and Gretel, Princess Florine in the Bluebird Pas de Deux from Sleeping Beauty, The Autumn Fairy from Cinderella, the Scarecrow in The Wizard of Oz, and Clara from the Nutcracker. She then went on to BYU-Idaho to receive her degree as a Registered Nurse. During nursing school she was a member of the Upper Valley Civic Ballet Company in Rexburg, Idaho where she performed in the ballet, Carmen. She has taught and assisted ballet for all ages for 9 years. Along with ballet, Marybeth has been trained in jazz, modern, tap, irish, folk, and contemporary dance. Marybeth Marybeth is married to Andy Wilde and they have two adorable daughters. She currently works at Utah Valley Hospital and she and her family live in Payson, Utah.

Ann Kotyk

Ann started her career at the age of 8 at The Academy of Ballet and has been dancing for a total of 16 years. She was taught under the direction of Carrie Write, Ann Royal and Phyllis Whitaker. She performed as the White Dove from Swan Lake. Ann has taken many forms of dance including jazz, modern, irish, tap, contemporary, and ballroom. She has assisted teaching for 4 years and is looking forward to soon taking on her own students. Ann graduated from Utah Valley University in Sign Laugage Interpreting, where she was a member of the campus dance team Synergy. Ann is married to Zack Kotyk and has an adorable little girl who occasionally makes her way to ballet class with her mom.
